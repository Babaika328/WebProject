ForeCastling: Swap What You Don’t Need

One-liner:

A web marketplace where users can trade items directly — no money involved, just exchanges between people.

Problem & Target Audience:

Many people own unused items but don’t want to sell or throw them away. ForeCastling helps users exchange belongings easily and fairly. Target audience: students, collectors, and budget-conscious users.

Core Features (MVP):

  • User accounts & authentication.
  • Post an item with photo, description, and estimated price.
  • Browse items with search and filters.
  • Propose or receive trade offers.
  • Dashboard to track ongoing and past trades.
  • Admin dashboard for moderation (remove scams/inactive listings).
  • Automation: email notification when someone offers a trade.

Povaridze

One-liner:

A community-driven platform where users vote on the best version of a dish by comparing different recipes for the same meal.

Problem & Target Audience:

People often find too many recipes for the same dish and can’t decide which one to follow. This platform helps food lovers discover the top-rated recipe for each dish. Target audience: home cooks, food bloggers, and recipe enthusiasts.

Core Features (MVP):

  • User registration & login.
  • Create and submit recipes for a dish.
  • Upvote/downvote system for ranking recipes.
  • Leaderboard showing top recipes per dish.
  • Comment section for feedback.
  • Search/filter recipes by dish name.
  • Automation: weekly email of “Top 5 trending dishes.”
